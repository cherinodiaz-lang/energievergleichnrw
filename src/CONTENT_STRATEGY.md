## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Style
- Professional but accessible
- Avoid jargon or explain it clearly
- Use active voice
- Address reader directly ("Sie")
- Provide practical, actionable advice

### Structure Template
1. **Kurzantwort** (3-5 sentences)
2. **Introduction** (1-2 paragraphs)
3. **Main Content** (3-5 sections with H2s)
4. **Key Points Summary** (bulleted list)
5. **FAQs** (3-5 questions with answers)
6. **Related Articles** (2-3 links)
7. **CTA Box** (link to Money Page)

### SEO Checklist
- [ ] Unique, compelling title (60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description (160 chars)
- [ ] Single H1 tag
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword in subheadings
- [ ] Internal links (3-5 total)
- [ ] External links (optional, 1-2)
- [ ] Images with alt text
- [ ] FAQ schema markup
- [ ] Word count 800-1,400
